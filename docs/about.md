# About Omniflow.id

Omniflow.id is an integrated ERP (Enterprise Resource Planning) solutions platform designed to help businesses in Indonesia and globally manage their operations efficiently. Founded in 2020, the company is headquartered at Jl. Sudirman No. 123, Jakarta Pusat, 10220, Indonesia.

## Modules

Omniflow.id offers a comprehensive suite of business modules:

1. **HRIS** - Human Resources Information System for managing employee data, payroll, attendance, and recruitment
2. **E-Commerce/Commerce** - Online store management and digital commerce solutions
3. **XRM** - Extended CRM for customer relationship management
4. **Accounting** - Financial management, bookkeeping, and reporting
5. **Analytics** - Business intelligence and data analytics dashboard
6. **URLs** - URL management and tracking
7. **Bizzcard** - Digital business card solutions
8. **PoS** - Point of Sale system for retail operations
9. **LMS** - Learning Management System for employee training and development
10. **Telemarketing** - Outbound sales and call center management
11. **Payment Gateway** - Integrated payment processing solutions

## Technology Stack

The Omniflow.id website is built with:
- **Frontend**: React + TypeScript
- **Build Tool**: Vite
- **Styling**: Tailwind CSS
- **Internationalization**: i18next
- **Icons**: Lucide React
- **Routing**: React Router
- **Deployment**: Vercel
